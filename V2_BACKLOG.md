# V2 Backlog

These are intentionally out of scope for V1. Revisit only after V1 has real usage signal.

- Accounts / sign-in. Anonymous shareable URLs with 30-day TTL are enough for the V1 hypothesis test.
- The link-orchestration canvas. No `/summer/[handle]` profile pages. No Spotify, Strava, or Instagram embeds.
- Multiple manifestos per user. One session, one manifesto. Re-do means start over.
- The "5 voices" feature. Three themes are the personalization. Voices are AI-rewrite-only in V1, single voice.
- Per-manifesto OG image generation. Static OG per theme is fine.
- Email reminders, weekly check-ins, or habit-tracker features. This is a ritual tool, not a habit tracker.
- A mobile app. PWA at best, web-first only.
- Custom hobby quit dates. September 21 is hard-coded. The constraint is the feature.
